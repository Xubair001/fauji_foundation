About Us

Fauji Foundation College for Girls in Rawalpindi stands as one of the city's respected educational institutions, committed to empowering young women through comprehensive academic programs.

We offer Pre-Med, Pre-Eng, ICS, Sc Gen & Humanities, preparing students for higher education and professional careers.

Our mission is to provide quality education with a focus on academic excellence, discipline, and character-building.

Our Programs

We offer a range of subjects across sciences, arts, and commerce, enabling students to pursue higher education and professional goals.

Our faculty comprises experienced educators dedicated to providing a high standard of education.

Students benefit from well-structured curricula, interactive teaching methodologies, and a conducive learning environment.
